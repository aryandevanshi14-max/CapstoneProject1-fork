About Us

Welcome to the Rajasthan Tourism Guide website, developed as part of our Capstone-I project. This platform is designed to provide users with information about Rajasthan’s famous tourist destinations, historic forts, beautiful temples, and cultural heritage sites.

Our aim is to create a simple and user-friendly travel guide that helps visitors explore important locations across Rajasthan with ease. The website includes destination details, images, map locations, ticket prices, and nearby hotel information to improve the overall travel experience.

Through this project, we combined web development concepts with practical implementation to build an informative and visually engaging tourism platform.
